About Us

Welcome to BCREC Music Club, located at Dr. B. C. Roy Engineering College in Durgapur, West Bengal, India. We are passionate about creating a vibrant and inclusive community for music enthusiasts of all genres.

Our club offers a platform for aspiring musicians to showcase their talent, organize events, and connect with like-minded individuals who share a love for music. Join us in celebrating the power of music and the joy it brings to our lives.
