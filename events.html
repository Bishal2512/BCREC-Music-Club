Upcoming Events!

Dr. B.C. Roy Engineering College proudly presents Swara Kala, a dynamic Music and Instrument competition. Led by coordinators Jahanvi Pandey and Arghyadeep Karmakar, this annual event transcends musical genres, uniting diverse talents in a harmonious celebration. Participants showcase their musical prowess, creating a vibrant tapestry of melodies. Join us at Dr. B.C. Roy Engineering College for an enchanting experience, where Swara Kala unfolds as a symphony of talent and creativity, bridging the gap between performers and appreciative audiences.
